## Core Positioning Insights

- Too many trainers do generic AI. Tomer must be highly specialised.
- Big institutes (DLH, HoT) already have generic training covered. Differentiate on results, not content.
- Unique positioning: you leave with a working agent you built yourself — not just knowledge.
- Combine LLMs with software engineering — this is what makes Tomer different from Kramer (law background, no technical depth).
- Verification-first approach: every output cites its source, every step logged, human checkpoint before anything is acted on.
- Luxembourg = reference country for compliance. Law firms and financial institutions under heavy regulation (CSSF, AML, AIFMD, UCITS). This is the sector, not a location tag.
- Target entry point at banks and financial institutions: Head of Compliance or Head of Legal — not HR.
- Management team angle: self-interest. They want to supervise and stay ahead of what their teams are doing.
- Not cheap. Not generic. Not Indian offshore. Bespoke, specialised, immediate results.
