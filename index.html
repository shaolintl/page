About

Most organisations know they need to do something about AI. Very few know where to start. That is the gap I fill.

I work as an AI transition manager and trainer for organisations of every kind, from public bodies, law firms and financial institutions to any team that wants AI it can actually trust. I come in for a defined period, usually one to two months, to solve a concrete AI problem: I assess honestly what AI should do for you, build the first trustworthy, human-supervised pipelines together with your team, train them as we go, and leave them able to run it without me. Because I have built production AI systems myself, including in regulated environments, I know what vendors will tell you, what they will not, and what to verify. I co-founded an AI company whose clients include a top-10 international law firm, I built a university Data Science programme from scratch, and I have led more than 600,000 euros of public research funding. When you want a longer relationship, I can continue on a fractional basis.

How I Help

Most organisations know they need to do something about AI. Very few know where to start. The vendors will answer that question, but in their own interest. I come in on your side of the table.

AI Transition Management and Fractional AI Officer

I come in for a defined period, usually one to two months, to solve a concrete AI problem and leave your team able to run it. Two ways to engage: a fixed price, or fixed costs plus a share of the savings I generate. When you want a longer relationship, I continue as your fractional AI officer afterwards.

AI Literacy and Training

I design and deliver AI literacy programmes for professional and public-sector teams with no prior AI background. Every programme is built around transparency, traceability and human oversight as working principles, and satisfies the AI literacy obligation under Article 4 of the EU AI Act with documented outcomes. 93% overall satisfaction rate.

Prompt-writing and AI pipelines

I build practical, human-supervised AI directly with your team: well-engineered prompts and supervised retrieval (RAG) in Claude, Gemini or Mistral, designed so people can trust the output and stay responsible for it. You keep the skill, not just a black box. Heavier on-premise production builds are delivered by my company, Enidia.

How I Work

Clear engagements, a low-risk way to start, and a fee model designed to be fair to you.

Start small. A short, fixed-fee assessment: I look at how you work today and show you where AI can take the load off, in transparent, trustworthy ways, with what each option would take and where the risks are. You get a clear map before committing to anything bigger.
Transition engagement. A defined period, usually one to two months, to solve a concrete AI problem: I build the first trustworthy, human-supervised pipelines with your team and leave them able to run it.
Stay on, if useful. When you want continuity, I continue as your fractional AI officer, without a full-time hire.
Two ways to agree the fee. A simple fixed price, or I share the risk and take a portion of the savings I create, measured against a baseline we agree together up front. Either way, you know what you are committing to before we begin.
Value you can measure. Where it matters, we measure the before and after on a real task, so the result is a number you can see, not a promise.
On site or remote. Based in Luxembourg, available across the region and beyond for the right engagement.
